Pilau recepi

Pilau is one of the most famous and most loved forms of rice dishes in East and Central Africa.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 table spoon of cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Take the two cups of rice and wash well with clean water.
  2. Take the 4 cups of water and boil it well to boiling point.
  3. Take the 1 table spoon of cooking oil, and add it to the boiling water.
  4. Take the 1 teaspoon of salt and also add it to the boiling water.
